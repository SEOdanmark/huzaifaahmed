Frequently Asked Questions

What is an Infographic?
An infographic is a visual representation of data or information, usually presented in the form of a chart or graph.
How does an Infographic help with SEO?
An infographic can be used to link to relevant content on your website and increase traffic from other websites that share your infographic. This helps boost visibility and rankings in search engine results pages (SERPs).
What are the benefits of creating an Infographic for SEO Link Building?
Creating an infographic gives you the opportunity to create creative visuals that capture attention, which can lead to increased engagement with your brand. Additionally, infographics can be shared across social media channels, giving you more opportunities to build links and gain exposure.
What steps are involved in creating an effective Infographic for SEO Link Building?
The steps involved in creating an effective infographic for SEO link building include researching topics related to your industry, developing a concept for the infographic, designing it using appropriate tools such as Photoshop or Canva, and promoting it through social media channels and other outlets.
What techniques should I use when creating my Infographic?
When creating your infographic you should consider factors such as design elements like colour palette, typography, imagery and flow; storytelling elements such as headline ideas, data visualization techniques; plus optimization tactics like using meta tags and ALT text for images. Additionally, consider including calls-to-action that encourage readers to visit your website or take further action after viewing the infographic.
